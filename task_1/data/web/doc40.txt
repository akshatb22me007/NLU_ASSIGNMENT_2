To my perpetual bewilderment, I am an Assistant Professor, in the  Department of Computer Science and Engineering , at the  Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) - Jodhpur .  I am also affiliated with the  Centre for Brain Science and Applications  and the  Centre for Education Technology  at IIT Jodhpur. [ CV ] It has been a while that I have been trying to understand and model how we do what we do and why we do what we do, towards the design of an embodied, social (~empathetic) "thinking machine". I am now all the more curious about the origin, evolution and development of fundamental cognitive abilities (e.g. language acquisition, aesthetic-sense, time-space continuum, spontaneous thought, contemplation) across all living species...  Of late I have beg un  wondering about the evolutionary origins of all forms of art ,  artistic behavior, learning, creativ ity, imagination  and sense of aesthetics, and how an amalgamation of all of these processes lead to embodied experiences....  I would love to be part of transdisciplinary science-writing/research initiatives/projects in any/all of these areas...  One can find a brief on my research interests and attempts  here , and on a few other issues that interest me,  here .   When at work, I should be somewhere around 26.4711° N, 73.1134° E.  If you would like to write to me, please address them to [rm<dot>banerjee<at>gmail<dot>com] or [romibanerjee<at>iitj<dot>ac<dot>in].  Thank you :-)...
