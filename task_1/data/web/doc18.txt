                        Doctoral Program
                     
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
   
 The Department of Mathematics hosts the Ph.D. degree Program. The Ph.D. Program at the Department of Mathematics, IIT Jodhpur is offered with the objective of tapping unique opportunities by evolving both motivated researchers in the frontline areas and a well-trained workforce in the form of teaching faculty. The Program strives to provide a vibrant environment to nurture the knowledge and interpersonal skills of the students in pursuit of developing a well-trained workforce. Further details about the doctoral program can be seen here     (Download file: 263 KB) . The selected candidates shall go through course works as per the institute's regulations and then a comprehensive exam will follow for which syllabus can be found here     (Download file: 89 KB) .
