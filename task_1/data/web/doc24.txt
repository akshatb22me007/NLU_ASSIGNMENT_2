                        Doctoral Program
                     
 
 
 
 
 Doctor of Philosophy Programs: Engineering Sciences Ph.D. Chemical Engineering Doctor of Philosophy in Chemical Engineering is focussed in a manner so that research scholar masters the different skills required for the research work, creatively shape and solve the complex problems, translate molecular information into new products and processes, generate new research ideas and IPR.
