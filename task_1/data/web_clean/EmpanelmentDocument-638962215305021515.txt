1. This notification and application have also been uploaded on the official website
of IIT Jodhpur http://www.iitj.ac.in.
2. The applications are invited online in prescribed proforma (as per Annexure-I)
from the Advocate(s) / Law Firm(s) possessing requisite qualifications and experience
(as per Annexure-II), Applications received through email shall only be entertained.
3. Eligible candidates must submit the application in prescribed proforma (as per
Annexure - I & II) along with an Undertaking (as per Annexure-III) through email to
registrar@iitj.ac.in and legalcell@iitj.ac.in in clear scanned single PDF format (not in
JPG/JPEG format, etc.) latest by 31st October, 2025.
4. The application(s) shall be scrutinized by the Institute.
5. Eligibility criteria of Advocate(s)/Law Firm(s) i.e.
(i) The Advocate or representative partner/member of the Law firm shall
possess a professional degree in Law from a recognized University/Institute
and should be enrolled as an Advocate with Bar Council.
(ii) Advocate(s)/Law Firm(s) to be eligible for empanelment shall have active
practice in the respective Court/Tribunals/Forums for at least 05 years at the
https://www.iitj.ac.in/main/en/iitj
INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY JODHPUR
bar and within the Jurisdiction of the Jodhpur.
(iii) The Advocate(s)/Law Firm(s) must have relevant experience and expertise
in Civil Cases, Criminal Cases, Service Laws, Labour Laws, Taxation
Laws, Consumer Forums, Public Premises & Eviction Laws and other Acts,
Rules and Regulations relating to educational Institutions, Service matters
or otherwise.
(iv) The Advocate(s)/Law Firm(s) should be well versed with the Institutes of
Technology Act, 1961/The Institutes of Technology (Amendment) Act
(2012) and the Statutes, Rules and Regulations framed there under.
(v) Advocate(s)/Law Firm(s) must have adequate infrastructure including
Office, Computer with e-mail facility, well maintained Law journal/library
etc.
(vi) Preference shall be given to Advocate(s)/Law Firm(s) having more relevant
experience and having exposure in dealing with matters on behalf of
educational Institute/University.
6. IIT Jodhpur shall conduct an interaction/interview/presentation of short-listed
Advocate(s) / Law Firm(s) fulfilling the eligibility criteria.
7. In respect of any clarification, the decision of IIT Jodhpur shall be final.
8. An Advocate(s) / Law Firm(s) will submit application for empanelment at IIT
Jodhpur. However, final decision for empanelment shall be at the discretion of IIT
Jodhpur.
9. The short-listed candidates shall be intimated on their Email Id for the purpose of
interaction on specific date/s.

12. Period, termination, resignation and expiry of term:
a. The Advocate(s)/Law Firm(s) shall be empaneled for the period of three
years which may further be extended on yearly basis at the discretion of the
Institute.
b. The counsel may resign from the empanelment by giving notice of
minimum period of three months.
c. The name of any Advocate/Law Firm may be removed from the panel at
the discretion of the Institute with immediate effect.
13. After the approval of committee, the final list of empaneled Advocate(s)/Law
Firm(s) will be prepared and intimated to the concerned.
14. The application should reach this office on or before 31st October, 2025, no
application shall be entertained, received beyond the prescribed date.
15. It may please be noted that incomplete/unreadable applications, application
submitted in other format and those who do not fulfill the requisite conditions during
interaction/interview/presentation shall summarily be rejected.
