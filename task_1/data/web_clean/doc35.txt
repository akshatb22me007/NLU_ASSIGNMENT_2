Who Am I?

I am a Professor of Computer Science at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Jodhpur, India. Earlier, I was a Professor at IIIT-Delhi. I received my Masters and PhD in Computer Science from the Lane Department of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at West Virginia University.
I was a member of the Indian Biometrics Standards Committee for e-gov applications. I also served as a member of the UIDAI's Biometrics Standard Subcommittee member (face, fingerprint, and iris standards). I also served as the Vice President (Publications) for IEEE Biometrics Council where I started IEEE Transactions on Biometrics, Behavior and Identity Science . See my introductory editorial for IEEE T-BIOM .
My current areas of interest are biometrics, image processing, machine learning, deep learning, image forensics, and information fusion.
