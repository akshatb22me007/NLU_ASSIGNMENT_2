Office Order
Subject: Appointment of Student Ombudsperson at IIT Jodhpur
In continuation to the Office Order No. IITJ/DIR/2024-25/09 dated 16 July 2024, in order to have an
external and independent system of addressing grievances from students, an external Ombudsperson is
hereby appointed. The external Ombudsperson will visit the Institute to interact with and address the
student(s) grievances, as and when required.
